## Executive Summary

### Key Findings
- Revenue growth slowed in recent months due to declining repeat purchases.
- Top 20% of customers generate ~80% of total revenue.
- Funnel drop-off is highest at the checkout stage.
- Older signup cohorts show significantly higher churn rates.
- Multiple abnormal revenue spikes correlate with promotional periods.

### Recommendations
- Prioritize retention campaigns for at-risk cohorts.
- Improve checkout UX to reduce funnel leakage.
- Launch VIP programs for high-LTV customers.
